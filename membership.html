Frequently Asked Questions

Can I try a class before joining?

+

Absolutely! We offer a free trial class for all new members. Simply contact us to schedule your free trial.

What do I need to bring to class?

+

For your first class, just wear comfortable athletic clothing. Once you join, you'll need a gi (uniform) which can be purchased at the gym.

Do you offer student discounts?

+

Yes! We offer a 15% discount for full-time students with valid student ID.

Is there a contract period?

+

No long-term contracts! All memberships are month-to-month with no joining fee. Cancel anytime with 30 days notice.
